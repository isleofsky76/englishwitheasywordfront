Rules

  • Think of a word, which can be a noun, verb, adjective, or any other type of word.
  • You can use up to 20 hints. No more hints will be provided after that.
  • If you do not guess the word within 20 questions, you lose the game.
  • Press "Submit Answer" to check if your guess is correct or incorrect.
  • Click "Show Answer" to reveal the correct answer.
  • Press "Restart Quiz" to start a new round.
